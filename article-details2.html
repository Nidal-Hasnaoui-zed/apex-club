Scientific Seminar: “Awareness Precedes Change”

By Ouisnaf Khalid | April 20, 2025

On Tuesday, March 18th, 2025, Apex Club organized a scientific seminar entitled “Awareness Precedes Change”, led by Dr. Bouchra Bekhat, as part of the club’s mission to promote intellectual growth and personal development among students. The seminar aimed to raise awareness about the importance of self-understanding, conscious decision-making, and continuous learning as key factors for positive change in both academic and personal life. This event reflected Apex Club’s dedication to creating educational spaces that inspire students to think critically and develop themselves holistically.

The seminar was rich in valuable information and meaningful guidance, addressing topics related to academic success, personal development, and life skills. Dr. Bouchra Bekhat shared insightful perspectives drawn from her expertise, offering students practical advice and motivational messages that resonated strongly with the audience. The event also encouraged active student participation, with engaging discussions, thoughtful questions, and open dialogue, creating an interactive and dynamic learning environment where students felt comfortable expressing their ideas and concerns.

The seminar concluded in a warm and appreciative atmosphere with the presentation of certificates of appreciation to Dr. Bouchra Bekhat, in recognition of her valuable contribution, and to the club supervisor, Mr. Youssef Al-Hedna, for his continuous support and dedication. This scientific seminar was a meaningful and enriching experience that left a positive impact on participants, reinforcing the belief that awareness is the first step toward meaningful and lasting change
